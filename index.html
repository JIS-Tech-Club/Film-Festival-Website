JIS Film Festival

About

The JIS Film Festival is open to all JIS High School students. If you are an aspiring filmmaker, actor or actress, cartoonist, or all-around film fan, chances are, you’ll find a role at the JIS Film Festival. Entries from IB Film students will be placed in the appropriate genre, competing with projects from filmmakers in non-IB courses, as well as with independent filmmakers.

Submissions

To be considered for the JIS Film Festival, your film must be an original production by you and your cast and crew, no longer than 7 minutes. The entry period is January 18 to February 8, 2016. Entries must not include plagiarized, unconsented, illegal or copyrighted material. Further rules will be made available soon.

Awards

Your film could win the prize for best in editing, cinematography, performance (actor and actress) or directing. Awards will also be given to the people’s choice, best of show, and best in genre.

Gala

Your film will be screened at a red-carpet event attended by a panel of judges from the independent film industry. Mark your calendars for February 17, 2016, and look out for paparazzi!
