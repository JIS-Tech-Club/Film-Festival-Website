Who can participate?

The JIS Film Festival is open to all JIS High School students. If you are an aspiring filmmaker, actor or actress, cartoonist, or all-around film fan, chances are, you’ll find a role at the JIS Film Festival. Entries from IB Film students will be placed in the appropriate genre, competing with projects from filmmakers in non-IB courses, as well as with independent filmmakers.

What are the rules?

To be considered for the JIS Film Festival, your film must be an original production by you and your cast and crew, no longer than 7 minutes, and submitted by January 31, 2016. Entries must not include plagiarized, unconsented, illegal or copyrighted material. All other rules are stated in the Official Film Festival Submission Form. Pick up a form today!

Can I help behind the scenes?

Yes! The Film Festival Coordinating Committe meets after school on Fridays in G01. Join us to learn more about the program, and to offer suggestions and support.
